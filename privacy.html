Adoro: Catholic Daily Prayer — Privacy Policy

Adoro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required. No tracking. No analytics.

Daily Readings body text is fetched from usccb.org. No user data is sent in this request.

Location Adoro requests your device location only when you use the Find My Parish feature. Your location is used solely to show nearby Traditional Latin Mass parishes. It is not stored, logged, or transmitted to any server.

Purchases In-app tip purchases are processed entirely by Apple App Store (iOS) or Google Play Billing (Android). Veridian LLC does not receive or store any payment card information.
